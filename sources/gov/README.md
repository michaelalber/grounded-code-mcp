# `gov` — Federal & Government Standards

This collection covers DOE/NNSA/LANL software engineering requirements, NIST cybersecurity
and AI frameworks, and federal compliance standards.

## What belongs here

- NIST SPs: SSDF (800-218), DevSecOps (800-204D), Zero Trust (800-207), Container Security (800-190)
- NIST AI frameworks: AI RMF (AI 100-1), Adversarial ML (AI 100-2), Synthetic Content (AI 100-4)
- NIST security control catalogs: 800-53r5, 800-53B, 800-171r3, 800-172
- NIST systems engineering: 800-160v1r1/v2r1, CSF 2.0, RMF (800-37r2)
- DOE Orders: 414.1D (Quality Assurance), Guide 414.1-4 (Safety Software), 10 CFR 830
- LANL engineering standards: ESM Chapter 21 (Software), supply chain risk (800-161r1)
- Pre-processed markdown summaries (files 00–12) and actionable templates for LANL/DOE projects
